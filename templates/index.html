PROJECTS

FUND RAISIN

Fund Raisin is a Web3 fundraising platform that connects digital artists and sponsors to create NFT-based charity campaigns. Artists submit exclusive art packs, and sponsors launch fundraising initiatives powered by those NFTs. Every NFT sold contributes directly to a social cause.

As the Project Manager, I led the development of the Creator Tools – a custom system that allows artists and sponsors to set up campaigns in just a few clicks. I coordinated the entire workflow between the design, development, and marketing teams, ensuring timely delivery and smooth feature rollouts.

For more information, visit www.fundraisin.app.
